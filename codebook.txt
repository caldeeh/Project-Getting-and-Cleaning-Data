Codebook for Getting and Cleaning Data project - Samsung phone activity tracker readings

"subjectid"
-ID number of the subject	
-values: 1-30

"activitytype"   
-which activity the subject was performing
-values: WALKING, WALKING_UPSTAIRS, WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS, SITTING, STANDING, LAYING      
                 
"tbodyacc mean x" 
-mean of the acceleration signal from the smartphone accelerometer X axis in standard gravity units 'g'

"tbodyacc mean y"                        
- mean of the acceleration signal from the smartphone accelerometer Y axis in standard gravity units 'g'

"tbodyacc mean z"          
- mean of the acceleration signal from the smartphone accelerometer Z axis in standard gravity units 'g'

"tgravityacc mean x"             
-mean of the gravitational force x axis

"tgravityacc mean y"                      
-mean of the gravitational force y axis

"tgravityacc mean z"                     
-mean of the gravitational force z axis
